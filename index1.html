Top 5 Movies According to Deepak

My Best Movies of All Time

1.IRON MAN

In the film, Tony Stark (played by Robert Downey Jr.), an industrialist and master engineer, builds a powered suit of armor and becomes the technologically advanced superhero, Iron Man. The movie follows his journey from being a playboy billionaire to a superhero, after he is kidnapped by a terrorist group and forced to build a weapon of mass destruction.

2.TRANSFORMERS

The story follows Sam Witwicky, played by Shia LaBeouf, a teenager who gets caught up in a war between the heroic Autobots and the villainous Decepticons, two factions of alien robots who can disguise themselves by transforming into everyday machinery, primarily vehicles1. The Autobots intend to retrieve and use the AllSpark, the powerful artifact that created their robotic race that is on Earth, to rebuild their home planet Cybertron and end the war, while the Decepticons have the intention of using it to build an army by giving life to the machines of Earth

3.BAT MAN:The Dark knight

The plot follows the vigilante Batman, police lieutenant James Gordon, and district attorney Harvey Dent, who form an alliance to dismantle organized crime in Gotham City1. Their efforts are derailed by the Joker, an anarchistic mastermind who seeks to test how far Batman will go to save the city from chaos

4.MISSION IMPOSSIBLE

In the film, Ethan Hunt (played by Tom Cruise) seeks to uncover who framed him for the murders of most of his Impossible Missions Force (IMF) team11. The film also stars Jon Voight, Henry Czerny, Emmanuelle Béart, Jean Reno, Ving Rhames, Kristin Scott Thomas, and Vanessa Redgrave

5.THE CHRONICLES OF NARNIA

The first film in the series, “The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe” (2005), follows the story of four children from the same family who have to leave their town because of the bombings of WWII2. While playing a game of hide-and-seek, the youngest member of the family, Lucy, finds a wardrobe to hide in. She travels back and forth into the wardrobe and finds a place named Narnia2. Here they realize that it was fated and they must now unite with Aslan to defeat an evil queen
